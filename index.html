About Sathi App

Sathi App is your friendly app for Type I Diabetic Patients to make their life easier. The app sends notification to the patient regarding timely blood sugar checkup and medication, to the local guardian if the patient misses the notification, and also to the doctor if the patient's blood sugar level goes extremely high so that timely action can be taken to avoid any bad consequences.

Personalized Notification for medication and checkup.

Dosage Alerts sent to Trusted Person and Medical Personnels.

Personalized Recommendations for Glucose level reading Timings.
